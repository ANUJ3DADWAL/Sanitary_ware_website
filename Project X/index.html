FEATURES OF WETX’S BATHROOM SANITARY ARE:

1. Complete Comfort

Jaquar bathroom sanitary items have been designed in a unique way that makes sure to go beyond just a sense of comfort. Not only that, it is ergonomically built to fit seamlessly in every type of bathroom design and cuts down roundtrips for access dramatically. Moreover, it can help in maintaining your bathroom’s wellness zone as well.

2. Sleek design

Aside from comfort, these sanitary ware solutions are made to naturally fit inside your bathroom without looking out of place. Jaquar has carefully designed sanitary ware for you with perfection to ensure that everything looks just right no matter what your bathroom size or structure is. Retain a powerful presence, while seeking no attention.

3. Premium Aesthetic

Jaquar, a premium sanitaryware company, provides the perfect aesthetics for a premium bathroom. Jaquar sanitary ware products come in monochrome colours that radiate a powerful presence with a gloss or matte finish. To the untrained eye, monochrome seems bleak to see, but true aesthetic lies in a simple yet eccentric idea. Our products are truly sleek but catch you off guard without intention. We transform your bathroom to be your oasis, cut off from the stress and worries of the outside world.

4. Anti germ finish

Unlike other sanitary ware options, Jaquar has an exclusive anti-germ finish. We care about you and your health, and strive to prioritize workplace hygiene and home hygiene at all times. Having clean and sanitized bathrooms can help give you an assurance of protection against germs & viruses that often plague our world. Jaquar provides one of the safest sanitaryware accessories like toilet wash basins, bathroom closets, and the best commodes in India that give you protection from any viruses or germs.
